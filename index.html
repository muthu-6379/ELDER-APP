Our Mission

We aim to provide a simple and efficient way for elderly individuals to request assistance with daily tasks, and for compassionate volunteers to offer their support.

"Our goal is to make it easy and convenient for elderly individuals to request help with everyday tasks, while offering a meaningful way for caring volunteers to lend their support."

How It Works

1. Register

Create an account as an elderly person needing help or as a volunteer.

2. Request/Offer Help

Elderly users can post requests for various tasks. Volunteers can browse and accept tasks.

3. Connect & Care

Once matched, connect with the volunteer or elderly person to arrange assistance.
